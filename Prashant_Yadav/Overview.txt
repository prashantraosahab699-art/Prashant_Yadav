Nexus Arena 2026 is a responsive esports tournament platform designed for Valorant, BGMI, and Counter-Strike 2 players. The platform enables users to browse tournaments, create and manage teams, track rankings, view match history, and participate in competitive gaming events. It includes user authentication, player profiles, leaderboards, tournament management features, and an administrative dashboard, providing a centralized environment for both players and tournament organizers. The project focuses on delivering a modern esports experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
